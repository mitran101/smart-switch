Why we built it

Most people have no idea what's actually on their energy bill. The numbers are there - unit rates, standing charges, levies - but no supplier explains what any of it means or whether it's normal.

That information gap is expensive. People stay on bad tariffs, miss overcharges, and have no basis to push back. We wanted to close it.

So we built a tool that does the reading for you. Drop your bill in. Ten seconds later you know exactly where your money is going - and whether any of it shouldn't be.
