Why It's Changing

The old system was criticised for being too restrictive and inconsistent, leaving some struggling families without help simply because their home wasn't classed as "expensive to heat." Removing this rule makes eligibility simpler, fairer, and automatic for those on qualifying benefits ‚Äî ensuring help reaches those most in need, but also increasing the burden on other customers who ultimately subsidise these discounts through their energy bills.
